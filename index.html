Elevate your environment with SCAD interior design

Combine expertise with high-tech resources to style spaces that wow

Interior designers enhance quality of life as they curate the places we love. At SCAD, interior design students join a 40-year, award-winning tradition of creating energetic, inspiring spaces. SCAD students explore a wide range of career paths, learning to maximize health, wellness, style, and sustainable practices across corporate, residential, retail, and hospitality design. Students capitalize on this wealth of contexts through SCADpro, the university's in-house creative consultancy, applying them to collaborations with corporate partners like Amazon, Chick-fil-A, Disney, and more.

Our mission

The interior design program prepares students to become responsible catalysts of change in the field of interior design. Effective communicators and innovative collaborators guided by professional ethics and values, SCAD graduates emerge as industry-ready, forward-thinking, purpose-driven designers and placemakers.

Our philosophy

The interior design program:

  • Offers unparalleled in- and out-of-class educational opportunities for students.
  • Utilizes SCAD's advanced teaching and learning resources and expertise to innovate and elevate the classroom experience.
  • Cultivates a diverse, inclusive, and respectful academic community that fosters social responsibility.
  • Maximizes opportunities for students and faculty to engage in interdisciplinary collaboration.
  • Expands students' career connections through engagement with SCAD alumni, mentors, and creative business professionals.
  • Amplifies students' professional communication skills (verbal, visual, and interpersonal) through engagement with SCADamp, the university's professional presentation studio.
  • Fosters students to be creative and ethical leaders who embrace sustainable practices.
  • Inspires students to elevate the human experience in the built environment through adaptive thinking and creative problem-solving.
  • Celebrates faculty and student interior design expertise through professional certification.

Related majors and minors

Interior design is an ever-evolving field. In order to thrive in such an industry, many students choose to pursue a double major or related minor while they are here. You can explore new perspectives, harness diverse creative approaches, and develop a unique, well-rounded style all while earning multiple degrees.
